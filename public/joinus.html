Our Constitution ::


The condition of media in India, which is called the fourth pillar of the democracy, is getting worse day by day! Even the journalists of the big media houses do not have the freedom to raise the voice of the public! Because of which the public of the country is making various allegations against the media. Journalists are being addressed with words like Dalal and Godi Media!

When the media of the country stops questioning the ruling party, then the fear of the ruling party goes away from the media, because it is the media that acts as a bridge between the public and the government! The media only works in taking the voice of the people to the government.

Today it is often seen that if a journalist somewhere in the country wants to expose any crime or corruption of a person with criminal tendencies in the government or any minister, then he is either imprisoned in any case or that journalist. is killed under some conspiracy. In such a situation, the media house in which that journalist is working, instead of supporting that journalist, that media house also tries to suppress those matters! Many such cases are coming to the fore every day in the country. Nowadays, politicians from small to big journalists have started thinking better to flatter them than to question them, because if the journalist asks the public questions from the leader, then that leader will not allow that journalist to come to him next time! The situation that is going on in the media in our country right now, not only the media, but the situation of the whole country seems to be getting worse! "Trust of Press Man" is working to bring all those true journalists of the country on one platform, who are not lagging behind in putting their lives in the work of reforming the society and the country through true journalism.

Driver society ::


The biggest oppressed and exploited society of the country is the driver society of India! Today the driver society of India, be it any kind of commercial driver, it has a very big role in the life of every citizen of the country in running the economy of India! Everywhere the driver is being exploited be it media outlets, auto taxi, cab, bus, truck trailer, jeep, tempo any kind of commercial driver! From the independence of India till today, in the interest of the driver, no such rule or law has been made by the central government and any state government till date, which can stop the injustice and atrocities on the driver! Today, the driver of the country is not able to run his family, there is a game of robbery going on with him everywhere, after leaving the road, it is not known whether he will come back home safely or not, he is shot dead and or somewhere else. Government servants are seen forcibly making illegal recovery from his pocket, as the driver is not one, he is not being heard in any department anywhere.,
"Trust of Press Man" is going to prove to be a boon for every driver of the country. With the Trust, many senior journalist colleagues from all corners of the country are connected to help each other, who are passionate about their honesty and true journalism. With whom we are able to raise the voice of every citizen of India. Those who are victims of injustice, atrocities and corruption somewhere!
